Dreaded Deep

Click here to go to the itch.io page!

Dreaded Deep was made for a 2 week game jam. You roam around a dark cave collecting artefacts while a hungry shark hunts you. With harpoons and a limited oxygen supply, can you collect all 6 artefacts to win?

A lot of credit goes to my friend Alex, who made almost the entire game. I unfortunately could not spend as much time as I would have liked on the game due to at home projects. However, I had a great time working on the sound design and implementation for the game.

As my first time using unreal, I focussed a lot on trying to understand how it works, especially in relation to the inspector. In the end, I was quite happy with how the sound system worked out, as I managed to make a system that could easily play layered sounds with distance fading, all from the inspector.
